    My name is Sai Kuppa. I am a technology leader with over 20 years of experience in the financial services industry. During my career, I have held multiple roles in both individual contributor and leadership capacity including software engineer, solutions architect, product manager and Director of engineering.

    After starting my career as Software Engineering at Citigroup, working 10+ years at Barclays Capital on Fixed Income front office trading platforms and 8+ more years at PGIM Investment Wealth Management systems, I possess in-depth understanding of financial products, business workflows related to order management systems, OTC Derivatives clearing and portfolio Investment/Risk management operations.

    During my career, I have successfully built and managed large scale high impact systems/platforms from the ground up. I have defined and delivered IT product roadmap focussing on UI/UX, API, Data Engineering, Cloud transformation, and DevOps automation programs and hired/led cross functional engineering teams spread across multiple global locations.

    My greatest strength is that I am equally at ease talking to executives, business users and engineers. I come from an engineering background, but I have a very strong understanding of the financial services business. This well rounded skill allows me to connect with users from disparate departments and points of view quickly and authentically. I also have the ability to see the big picture and execute strategy at a high level, while not losing sight of the nuts and bolts and understanding the execution difficulties at an engineering level.

    Outside of work, I spend time travelling with my family, coaching coding, mathematics, swimming, and tennis for my kids. I currently intend to continue to work in technology but across a broader range of industries and with a greater focus on leadership opportunities.
